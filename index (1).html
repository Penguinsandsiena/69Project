Campus Life

School Church

Was Established in 1883

Star of the Sea College is a Catholic school for girls. This is the church were mass is held every once a fortnight and some extras for special events.



Senior Campus

Was Established in 1883

This area is mainly for the senior students of the school to spend during snack and lunch time. It is a calm and easy area for the students to rest and hang out with one another.



Performing arts room

Was Established in 1883

The performing arts room is a class students do for a few years, it helps them express themselves by acting and dressing up. It is a great way for students to make friends by being grouped up into pairs or small groups for warm ups and the activites.
